Our Services

StudyFind helps you move from first ideas to a concrete study plan in Denmark through tailored guidance and preparation. This demo uses an AI chatbot connected to a custom dataset of bachelor programs.

Workshop

Interactive online sessions where you learn how the Danish higher education system works, how to use application portals, and how to compare study paths.

Exam Preparation

Support for language and admission tests, with practice materials and simple study plans tailored to your timeline.

Choice Advising

One-to-one guidance to match your interests, budget and language preferences with bachelor options in Denmark.

For Students

Use the chatbot to explore programs by field, city, language and tuition. Try questions like “Bachelor in Business in English near Copenhagen for EU students”.

For Institutions

Universities can use a similar assistant to present their programs in a clear, comparable way to international applicants, answering questions 24/7.

About

StudyFind is a demo built for an “AI for Business” course. It combines a curated dataset of Danish bachelor programs with an AI assistant that understands natural language questions.
